Intro

A little about myself.

Welcome to my page. My name, as the title says, is Joseph Panetta. Im a recent graduate from Lambda School Online for full stack Web development. A serious career shift for me as you will discover in my Work And Professional outliner.

I have been called a jack of all trades,but I'd like to think Of it as, lets say, well rounded. How much can you know if you stop trying to know. I'm the kind of person that wont really feel comfortable doing anything unless i read the book and talked to everyone i could, and researched every possible avenue. I believe that kind of perserverence combined with a good solid education makes me a valuable addition to any dev team.

Work

My first job experences were with family owned pizza shops when i was around 12. aside from the issues with my knees I have had a job, sometiomes more than one , ever since. The restaurant business took care of everything I could want. Untill I started the family. I had to start looking for employment with benefits and the like. That led me to Giant Eagle Grocery Store in 2009 where I was hired as a meat cutters apprentice.
I completed the appenticeship in 6 months and continued on there untill 2011 when i started to have knee problems that I had to have multiple surgeries for. I started searching for a new line of work after 9 months of FMLA. I found another job as a medical gas systems specialist at compliant healthcare technologies in Independence Ohio. Paid very well and I loved the job.i ewas responsible for any gas system in a hospital from the source to the room its used in. This included pressure and flow checks , alarm testing anbd any repairs or replacements as needed

In 2014 my knees took a serioous turn for the worse. I almost lost a,y leg. and was off work again.. And here today after a few years of recovery and some serious thought i attended a software developer boot camp and im now ready to serve the worlds software needs.
